Metro Exodus is released in 2019 for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, Xbox One and Stadia and in 2020 for Amazon Luna. A Linux and macOS conversion was released in 2021. An enhanced version of the game released for Microsoft Windows on May 6, 2021 and was later released for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X/S on June 18, 2021. The game received positive reviews from critics.
